Résumé

Rakshit Pradhan is an undergraduate at Purdue University studying physics. He is passionate about machine learning, its applications, and data analysis. He currently researches robotic grasp prediction at Purdue's IDEAS Lab, building multimodal vision models over RGB, depth, and segmentation inputs. Previously, he interned in growth engineering at Wondercraft (YC S'22) and worked on model optimization at PerspectAI. His background in physics keeps him close to the math; his computer science minor keeps the pipelines clean.
